OUR MISSION

Why Choose Us?

We endeavor to facilitate a path to wellbeing from a holistic perspective, that would honor trust and confidentiality.

Our carefully vetted team of professionals will initiate the therapeutic connection with sincerity and integrity, encouraging a dialogue that would establish rapport. One of our key goals is to increase your emotional and ego strength to resolve conflicts, validate your emotions and learn to structure a cognitive scaffolding to exit from cognitive thinking traps.

Your self-esteem and your individual profile would be our map to facilitate emotional well-being and psychoeducation, to cope with conflicts beyond the therapy sessions. It is important to note that we have the expertise, the experience, the education, and credentials, to provide the degree of individualised attention that would facilitate change.

Core Features

We Offer Therapy, Workshops & Rooms

Individual therapy & counselling

For children, adolescents, and adults (In person and online)

Group Therapy

Group therapy sessions provide a safe medium whereby, in a confidential environment, clients can validate their emotional conflicts and can learn from each other coping mechanisms with empathy and social intelligence. Eventually, support groups may emerge for the more extended period ahead. It is therapeutic in itself.

Relationship & Marriage Therapy

Couple therapy can help you and your partner improve your relationship if you are having communication and mis communication issues. Relationship difficulties can be resolved by helping you and your partner identify feelings generated by the discord and identify the thoughts and learn the skills to diffuse emotionally loaded thoughts, understanding each other’s perspectives and allowing them to see one another with empathy and compassion.

Workshops

  1. Schools (Teachers, management, students, and parents)
  2. Corporate (Time management, leadership development etc.)

Clinical Assessments

Life Skills

Our approach to personal development and growth-focused life skills primarily integrates evidence-based methodologies, such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), along with other promising approaches, including psychodynamic, humanistic, systemic, and somatic therapies. These frameworks are designed to enhance critical thinking, emotional intelligence, and social intelligence while fostering essential competencies such as decision-making, stress management, planning and organization, conflict resolution, negotiation, and leadership. By equipping individuals with these skills, we empower them to navigate personal and professional challenges with confidence and resilience.

A co-working space for Mental Health Professionals

At AARC, We also offer spaces to qualified psychologists, counsellors, therapists, psychotherapists, life coaches, and other complementary health and wellness experts, especially those who need a private conference room to discuss with and assist their clients. Our modern facility offers a combination of permanent and hot desks, as well as access to four private conference rooms in different sizes, each room includes space for all kinds of therapy, a client waiting area, a small event area, a shared kitchen, and other necessary office amenities like high-speed wi-fi, filing cabinets and office stationery.

Significantly, AARC is a self-service environment with limited administrative and in-person support. To meet the needs of our members, we rely on technology and effective systems and procedures. Professional Therapists will also have the facility of flexible working hours, so they can choose the time that best suits their productivity. They can enjoy more flexibility in booking times, if the rooms are booked in advance, charges can vary depending on the booking hours and timings. All these facilities in comfortable, secure and well designed rooms.
